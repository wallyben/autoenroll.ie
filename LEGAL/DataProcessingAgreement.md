# Data Processing Agreement

This DPA forms part of the Terms between Customer (Controller) and AutoEnroll.ie (Processor).

## Subject Matter
Processing payroll data solely to validate files, assess auto-enrolment eligibility, and generate reports.

## Duration
Processing occurs during subscription term; zero-retention applied by default with optional short retention when enabled.

## Obligations of Processor
- Process data only on documented instructions.
- Maintain confidentiality and ensure personnel are bound by confidentiality obligations.
- Implement appropriate technical and organisational measures (see SecurityOverview).
- Assist Controller with data subject rights and DPIAs.
- Delete or return personal data at end of provision unless law requires storage.

## Subprocessors
Stripe (billing), hosting providers (Vercel, Fly/Render, object storage). Controller will be notified of additions.

## International Transfers
Transfers outside EEA rely on appropriate safeguards (SCCs or equivalent) provided by subprocessors.

## Audit
Processor will provide audit reports or permit audits under confidentiality obligations with reasonable notice.
